The good life

Townhomes on Cantey is a Class A complex featuring 12 three-bedroom/two-and-a-half bath townhomes adjacent to the Texas Christian University (TCU) campus. Included in each townhome are walk in closets, hardwood floors, granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, large living spaces, and a cleaning service. Each unit also features a security system, a study area, private balcony, and a two car garage with additional covered parking if needed.
